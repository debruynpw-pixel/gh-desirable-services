Who We Are

Born in Kagiso.
Built for Gauteng.

GH Desirable Services was founded in 2017 by Gift Mpho Holoein in Kagiso Ext 12, Krugersdorp. What started as a passion for youth culture became a full-service creative powerhouse — clothing, textiles, branding, and printing under one roof.

Our flagship brand, Citayanos, is a lifestyle clothing line that embodies music and entertainment — designed for the bold, the creative, and the ambitious youth of South Africa.

We're CIPC registered, SARS tax compliant, and trusted by individuals, startups, corporates, and sports clubs across the West Rand and beyond.
